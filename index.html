About Me

I am a Wallenberg-NTU Postdoctoral Fellow at the Division of Information Technology and Operations Management, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, hosted by Prof. Boh Wai Fong. My fellowship is funded by the Knut and Alice Wallenberg Foundation.

My research focuses on software and systems engineering, with particular emphasis on the human and management aspects of these disciplines. More recently, in response to the growing integration of artificial intelligence into software and systems engineering practices, I have developed an interest in human-centered AI for software and systems engineering.

I completed my PhD at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden (2025), in the Engineering Design Department, School of Industrial Technology and Management. I was supervised by Assoc. Prof. Fredrik Asplund and Prof. Martin Törngren. I was also an affiliated PhD student of the Wallenberg AI, Autonomous Systems and Software Program (WASP) Graduate School. In Autumn 2023, I was a Visiting Student Researcher at the UC Berkeley Center for Long-Term Cybersecurity, supported by the Karl Engvers Foundation.

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss potential research collaboration. Please feel free to contact me.
